Why Take a Probiotic Complex?

A good probiotic should include multiple strains—like L. acidophilus and B. bifidum—to target digestion, immunity, and mental health. It helps rebalance gut flora and reduce bloating.

What to Look For

  • At least 10 billion CFUs
  • Enteric-coated capsules
  • Strains specific for your need (e.g. L. rhamnosus for anxiety)

Conclusion
