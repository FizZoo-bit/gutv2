What Is the Gut-Brain Axis?

The gut-brain axis is a two-way communication network between your gut and brain, linked through hormones, nerves, and bacteria. 90% of serotonin is made in the gut!

Signs It’s Off Balance

  • Anxiety or depression without clear triggers
  • Brain fog and difficulty focusing
  • Digestive problems + emotional ups and downs

How to Support It

  • Probiotics: Look for strains like L. rhamnosus and B. longum for mood support
  • Anti-inflammatory foods: Omega-3s, leafy greens, berries
  • Mindful practices: Yoga, journaling, vagus nerve stimulation

Closing Thoughts
