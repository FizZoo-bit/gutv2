Restoring Gut Flora Naturally

Why Gut Flora Matters

Gut flora—or gut microbiota—are trillions of bacteria that help digest food, support immunity, and produce key nutrients. Antibiotics, sugar, and stress can kill the good ones, letting harmful strains take over.

How to Rebalance Naturally

  • Eat Fermented Foods: Add kefir, sauerkraut, kimchi, and kombucha daily.
  • Fiber-Rich Prebiotics: Oats, bananas, asparagus, and chicory root help good bacteria thrive.
  • Take a Probiotic: Multi-strain probiotics like this recommended brand replenish your gut.
