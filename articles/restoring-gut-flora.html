Restoring Gut Flora Naturally

Learn how to repopulate your gut flora with natural methods including fermented foods, prebiotics, and stress reduction.

Why Gut Flora Matters

The gut microbiome helps with digestion, immunity, and mood regulation. A disrupted microbiome can lead to disease.

Top Prebiotic Foods

Onions, garlic, bananas, and asparagus feed healthy bacteria in the gut.

Fermented Foods to Include

Sauerkraut, kefir, kimchi, and yogurt reintroduce beneficial microbes.

Lifestyle Tips

Sleep well, reduce stress, and avoid antibiotics unless absolutely necessary.

Recommended Supplements

  • Probiotic Complex – microbiome balance
  • L-Glutamine – gut lining repair
  • Digestive Enzymes – improved digestion
