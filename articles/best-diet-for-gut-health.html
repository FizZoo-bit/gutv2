Best Diet for Gut Health

Why Diet Matters

Your gut bacteria thrive on certain foods—and suffer with others. Choosing a gut-friendly diet helps eliminate symptoms like bloating, gas, and irregularity.

Top Diet Plans

  • Low FODMAP: Reduces fermentable carbs—great for IBS relief
  • Anti-Inflammatory: Focuses on omega-3s, leafy greens, spices
  • Whole Foods Based: Ditches processed foods for fiber-rich meals

What Works Best?

Everyone’s gut is different. Keep a journal to track which foods trigger symptoms. Simplicity often wins.
