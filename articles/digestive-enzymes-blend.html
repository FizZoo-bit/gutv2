What Are Digestive Enzymes?

Digestive enzymes break down carbs, fats, and proteins so your body can absorb nutrients. If you’re bloated after meals, low enzymes may be to blame.

Key Enzymes to Look For

  • Amylase: Breaks down carbs
  • Lipase: Breaks down fats
  • Protease: Breaks down proteins
